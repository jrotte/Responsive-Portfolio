Overview



John attended The Ohio State University as an honors student and received a BSBA in Finance upon graduation.

Prior to diving into the tech industry and enrolling at Northwestern University, John worked in both aerospace and investment management after college.

Such opportunities have afforded him the ability to work in a variety of capacities all over the U.S. and internationally among great teams and cultures.

Outside of his passion for technology and innovation, some of John's favorite pastimes include hiking, running, and cooking (which is still a work in progress).
